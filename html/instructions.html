Vegan Vickie!

Holy guacamole!!!
Vegan Vickie's worst nightmare is coming true, meat is falling from the sky... Can you help her escape the meatfall and save the veggies along the way?

Instructions:
  • Use your arrow keys to move Vickie left and right...
  • Vegetables are like the best, so like as they fall from the sky try to collect them. They will disapear after you have collected them.
  • Make it from one side of the sunflower field to the other without running into any meat to win!
  • If you hit a piece of meat, game over... Vickie hasn't ate meat in over 20 years, even if it touches her skin she won't have the strength to make it through the storm.
